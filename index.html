Who is this man?

On May 19, 1870 a man named Hamilton Howard "Albert" Fish was born. Although he might look frail, dull, and even too grey and old man like to be serial killer, he is indeed one of the most notorious serial killers of his time. Know for the gruesome acts of rape, molestation, and cannibalism he took out upon children.
